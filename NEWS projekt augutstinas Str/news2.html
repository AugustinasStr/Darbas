Student, 10, wins national coding competition

29 July 2024

A Wiltshire school pupil says he is "proud" to have won a national coding competition. Wilf, 10, from Wansdyke Primary School in Devizes, took joint first prize in Discovery Education 2024 Summer Coder Challenge. He designed a racing game using block coding to earn top prize at the popular computer programming competition for UK schools. Wilf's teacher Jennifer Murray said: "Block coding has been fantastic for showing the students how their favourite technology is created and having a go for themselves." "We hope it inspires more future coders, just like Wilf," she added.

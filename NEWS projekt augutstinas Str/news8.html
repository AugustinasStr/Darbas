Coding: Female engineers deleting myth that tech is for men

4 December 2022

"I foolishly thought coding was for men good at maths." Zoe Thomas, 29, from Caerphilly, felt she needed a new challenge but never thought she could work with computers. But after taking evening classes in coding she switched careers from customer service to software engineering for an insurance firm. Latest figures from the Office for National Statistics show that women make up 31% of staff in the wider technology industry. But for software developers, web design professionals and data analysts, the proportion is far lower at just 18%.

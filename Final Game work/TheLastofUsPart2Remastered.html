The Last of Us Part 2 Remastered

Description

The Last of Us Part II Remastered is a third-person action-adventure survival horror video game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is a remastered version of the 2020 game The Last of Us Part II, featuring visual and technological improvements, new modes and content, scrapped levels, developer commentaries, expanded accessibility options, and more. It was released on January 19, 2024, for PlayStation 5 and is expected to release for Microsoft Windows on April 3, 2025.
